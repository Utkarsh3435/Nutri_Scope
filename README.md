🥗 NutriScope – Smart Food Safety Assistant

📌 Overview
NutriScope is a smart application designed to help users make safe and informed decisions while buying packed food products.
It focuses on detecting allergens, analyzing nutritional value, and providing personalized health insights in real-time.

🚨 Problem Statement

Nowadays, many people have various food allergies, but they face serious issues at the time of buying packed food products.
Ingredient labels are often complex and difficult to understand, leading to unintentional consumption of harmful allergens.

This creates serious health risks, and currently, there is no quick and reliable solution to check product safety instantly.

💡 Solution

NutriScope solves this problem by allowing users to scan food products and instantly analyze ingredients based on their personal health profile.

It ensures users can decide before they buy, not after they suffer.

🚀 Key Features
🔍 1. Allergy Scan
Scan packed food products using barcode or image
Detect allergen ingredients instantly
Personalized results based on user allergy profile

⚖️ 2. BMI Scan
Calculates user BMI (Body Mass Index)
Provides health category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, etc.)
Helps in making better dietary choices

🔄 3. Alternate Food Recommendation
Suggests safer alternative products
Recommends options without harmful allergens
Helps users switch to healthier choices

🧠 4. Future Health Tips
Provides personalized health suggestions
Based on user profile and consumption patterns
Promotes long-term healthy habits

⚖️ 5. Product Comparison
Compare two packed food products
Analyze nutritional values and ingredients
Helps users choose the healthier option

🏗️ System Architecture (High Level)
1)User scans product (barcode/image)
2)Ingredient data is fetched from database/API
3)System matches ingredients with user allergy profile
4)Output is generated:
   Safe / Unsafe alert
   Ingredient breakdown
   Recommendtion
  
🛠️ Tech Stack (Example – you can modify)
Frontend: React / Flutter
Backend: Node.js / Django
Database: MongoDB / Firebase
APIs: Food ingredient & nutrition APIs
AI/ML (Future): Ingredient prediction & recommendation system
